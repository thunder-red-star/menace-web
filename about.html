About this app

This app serves as a computer-based simulation of MENACE, so one doesn't have to obtain 700 matchboxes, or play hundreds of games manually, to understand how MENACE works. Provided are pages to play your games against a MENACE-style AI, a page to train MENACE against itself (faster than playing games against it manually), a page to load a preset weighting for MENACE's "matchboxes", and a page to analyze MENACE's weightings and view graphs, as well as dump weightings.


This app uses your LocalStorage as a database, so if you exit the page, your weightings will be saved. However, on the train page, you can clear your weightings, so you can train against a new set of games.


On the training page, you can configure how MENACE trains itself. Many configuration variables are available, including rewards/punishments, initial weights, and more.


This version of MENACE is meant to be more versatile than the original, being able to play both X and O. However, the concepts of "matchboxes" determining moves is still the same -- there are just more matchboxes.
